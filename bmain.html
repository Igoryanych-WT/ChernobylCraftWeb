ChernobylCraft - global modification for Minecraft Bedrock (MCPE), on the subject of the Chernobyl exclusion zone in the universe S.T.A.L.K.E.R. The modification is a global Add-on and contains many elements from the game universe S.T.A.L.K.E.R. as NPC, mutants, interface and elements of the environment from the exclusion zone.

STALKER atmosphere

Feel the atmosphere of the exclusion zone of the game STALKER in Minecraft.

NPCs and mutants

The modification contains many entities from the S.T.A.L.K.E.R. game.

Factions: Stalkers, Bandits, Military, Duty, Freedom, Mercenaries, Ecologists, Monolith.

Many mutants from the trilogy of S.T.A.L.K.E.R.!
