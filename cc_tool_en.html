ChernobylCraft SandBox Tool - part of the modification of ChernobylCraft, which is Add-on for the Bedrock edition of Minecraft. The addon is a kind of designer for the player. With his help, the player can independently recreate the world of the zone of alienation of the universe S.T.A.L.K.E.R. in its own image and taste.
Addon transforms the world of Minecraft with such innovations as several types of weapons from S.T.A.L.K.E.R., NPC and mutants, an updated game interface and high-quality textures of the environment and objects.
