ChernobylCraft - global modification for Minecraft Bedrock(MCPE), on the theme of the Chernobyl exclusion zone on the universe of S. T. A. L. K. E. R. Modification is a global Add-on using ScriptingAPI and contains many elements from the game universe S. T. A. L. K. E. R. as NPCs, mutants, the interface and the elements of the environment in the exclusion zone.
